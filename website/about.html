Who We Are

Our Story

PaMarket was founded in 2026 with a bold and simple mission: to make buying and selling in Zimbabwe accessible to everyone. We saw how hard it was for ordinary Zimbabweans to connect with buyers and sellers without paying listing fees or relying solely on word of mouth.

So we built PaMarket, a digital marketplace designed from the ground up for Zimbabwe. Whether you are selling a phone, renting a room, or looking for your next vehicle, PaMarket puts you in front of active buyers and sellers every day, at no charge.

Today, PaMarket covers 25 cities and towns across Zimbabwe, with thousands of listings across 12 categories. We are proud to be the platform Zimbabweans trust.
