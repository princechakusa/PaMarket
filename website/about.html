About PaMarket

Zimbabwe's #1 Free Classified Marketplace — built by Zimbabweans, for Zimbabweans.

Who We Are

Our Story

PaMarket was founded in 2024 with a single, bold mission: to make buying and selling in Zimbabwe as simple, safe, and free as possible. We saw how hard it was for ordinary Zimbabweans — from Harare's bustling suburbs to smaller towns like Gweru, Masvingo, and Chinhoyi — to connect with buyers and sellers without paying costly listing fees or relying on word of mouth.

So we built PaMarket — a digital marketplace designed by Zimbabweans, for Zimbabweans. Whether you're selling a second-hand phone, renting out a property, or looking for your next vehicle, PaMarket puts you in front of thousands of active buyers and sellers every single day — completely free of charge.

Today, PaMarket covers 25+ cities and towns across Zimbabwe, with tens of thousands of listings spanning property, vehicles, electronics, jobs, fashion, and more. We're proud to be the platform Zimbabweans trust to power their commerce.
