Who We Are

Our Story

PaMarket was founded in 2026 by Prince Chakusa with a single, bold mission: to make buying and selling in Zimbabwe as simple, safe, and free as possible. Prince saw firsthand how hard it was for ordinary Zimbabweans — from Harare's bustling suburbs to smaller towns like Gweru, Masvingo, and Chinhoyi — to connect with buyers and sellers without paying costly listing fees or relying solely on word of mouth.

So he built PaMarket — a digital marketplace designed by Zimbabweans, for Zimbabweans. Whether you're selling a second-hand phone, renting out a property, or looking for your next vehicle, PaMarket puts you in front of thousands of active buyers and sellers every single day — completely free of charge. No listing fees. No commissions. No barriers.

Today, PaMarket covers 25+ cities and towns across Zimbabwe, with tens of thousands of listings spanning property, vehicles, electronics, jobs, fashion, and more. We're proud to be the platform Zimbabweans trust to power their commerce — and we're just getting started.

Core Values

The principles that guide how we build, behave, and serve Zimbabwe

Integrity

We hold ourselves and our community to the highest standards of honesty. We moderate every listing, verify sellers, and work hard to ensure every interaction on PaMarket is trustworthy and safe. When we say free, we mean free. When we say your data is safe, we mean it.

PaMarket is more than a marketplace — it's a community. We celebrate the Zimbabwean spirit of enterprise and hustle. We work to lift every member through commerce, from small-scale sellers in Mbare to large property agencies in Borrowdale. Together we grow.
