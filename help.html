Getting Started
How do I create an account?+
Sign up using your email address or continue with Google. Simply click "Sign In" in the top header, then select "Register." Fill in your name, email, phone number, and a password. Your account will be ready instantly — no email verification required to start browsing.
Is PaMarket free to use?+
Yes — posting and browsing on PaMarket is always 100% free. There are no hidden fees, no listing costs, and no commission on sales. We offer optional paid promotions (Featured and Sponsored listings) to increase visibility, but these are entirely optional.
What areas does PaMarket cover?+
PaMarket covers all of Zimbabwe, including 25+ cities and towns: Harare, Bulawayo, Chitungwiza, Mutare, Gweru, Kwekwe, Kadoma, Masvingo, Hwange, Chinhoyi, Norton, Marondera, Ruwa, Chegutu, Zvishavane, Bindura, Beitbridge, Victoria Falls, Kariba, and more.
Posting Ads
How do I post an ad?+
Click the amber "Post Ad" button in the header. Choose your category, fill in your listing title, description, price, city, and contact number, then add photos. Once submitted, your ad will be reviewed by our team and usually goes live within 24 hours.
Why was my ad rejected?+
Ads must follow our Listing Rules. Common reasons for rejection include: inaccurate or misleading descriptions, prohibited items (weapons, counterfeit goods, drugs), duplicate listings, or missing key details. You can resubmit after making corrections.
How long do ads stay active?+
Ads are active for 90 days from the date of approval. After 90 days they expire automatically. You can repost the same ad for free to keep it visible. Promoted (Featured or Sponsored) ads have the same 90-day period but receive significantly more visibility during that time.
Can I post multiple ads?+
Yes! You can have up to 10 active listings per account at any time. If you need more, please contact us at support@pamarket.co.zw to discuss your needs — we're happy to help businesses manage larger catalogues.
Account & Safety
How do I get verified?+
Verified status signals to buyers that you're a trusted seller. To get verified, go to your Dashboard, Profile Settings, and follow the identity verification steps. You'll need to submit a valid Zimbabwe national ID or passport. Verification is reviewed within 1–2 business days.
How do I report a suspicious listing?+
On any listing page, scroll down to find the "Report this listing" link. Select the reason for reporting and submit. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email support@pamarket.co.zw directly.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it?+
Click "Sign In" in the header, then click "Forgot Password?" on the sign-in form. Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a reset link. The link expires after 1 hour for security. If you don't receive an email within a few minutes, check your spam folder.
How do I stay safe when meeting buyers or sellers?+
Always meet in a public place during daylight hours. Never pay in advance without verifying the item. Bring a friend for high-value transactions. Do not share personal financial details. If something feels suspicious, trust your instincts and report the listing.
Payments & Promotions
How much does a Featured listing cost?+
Featured listings are $5 per listing per month. Sponsored listings are $15 per listing per month. Contact us at support@pamarket.co.zw or WhatsApp +263 712 345 678 to activate a promotion for your ad.
Does PaMarket take a commission on sales?+
No. PaMarket is a free platform for connecting buyers and sellers. We do not facilitate or take commission on transactions between users. All payments are handled directly between buyer and seller. We are not responsible for any financial disputes between users.
What payment methods do you accept?+
For promotional packages, we accept EcoCash, OneMoney, Zimswitch, and USD bank transfer. Contact our sales team at support@pamarket.co.zw for payment details. We're working on bringing more payment options to the platform soon.
