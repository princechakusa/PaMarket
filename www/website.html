About PaMarket

Our Story

PaMarket was founded in 2024 with a single, bold mission: to make buying and selling in Zimbabwe as simple, safe, and free as possible. We saw how hard it was for ordinary Zimbabweans — from Harare's bustling suburbs to smaller towns like Gweru, Masvingo, and Chinhoyi — to connect with buyers and sellers without paying costly listing fees or relying on word of mouth.

So we built PaMarket — a digital marketplace designed by Zimbabweans, for Zimbabweans. Whether you're selling a second-hand phone, renting out a property, or looking for your next vehicle, PaMarket puts you in front of thousands of active buyers and sellers every single day — completely free of charge.

Today, PaMarket covers 25+ cities and towns across Zimbabwe, with tens of thousands of listings spanning property, vehicles, electronics, jobs, fashion, and more. We're proud to be the platform Zimbabweans trust to power their commerce.

🚀 Getting Started
How do I create an account?+
Sign up using your email address or continue with Google. Simply click "Sign In" in the top header, then select "Register." Fill in your name, email, phone number, and a password. Your account will be ready instantly — no email verification required to start browsing.
Is PaMarket free to use?+
Yes — posting and browsing on PaMarket is always 100% free. There are no hidden fees, no listing costs, and no commission on sales. We offer optional paid promotions (Featured and Sponsored listings) to increase visibility, but these are entirely optional.
How do I download the app?+
The PaMarket app is available on both Google Play (Android) and the App Store (iOS). Search for "PaMarket Zimbabwe" or scan the QR code on our homepage. The app gives you push notifications when buyers contact you — so you never miss a sale.
What areas does PaMarket cover?+
PaMarket covers all of Zimbabwe, including 25+ cities and towns: Harare, Bulawayo, Chitungwiza, Mutare, Gweru, Kwekwe, Kadoma, Masvingo, Hwange, Chinhoyi, Norton, Marondera, Ruwa, Chegutu, Zvishavane, Bindura, Beitbridge, Victoria Falls, Kariba, and more.
📝 Posting Ads
How do I post an ad?+
Click the orange "Post Ad" button in the header or the bottom navigation bar. Choose your category, fill in your listing title, description, price, city, and contact number, then add photos. Once submitted, your ad will be reviewed by our team and usually goes live within 24 hours.
Why was my ad rejected?+
Ads must follow our Listing Rules. Common reasons for rejection include: inaccurate or misleading descriptions, prohibited items (weapons, counterfeit goods, drugs), duplicate listings, or missing key details. You'll receive a notification if your ad is rejected and can resubmit after making corrections.
How do I edit or delete my ad?+
Go to your Dashboard by clicking your avatar in the top right corner, then navigate to "My Ads." You'll see all your active and pending listings. Click "View" to see the listing or "Delete" to remove it. Note that once deleted, the listing cannot be recovered.
How long do ads stay active?+
Ads are active for 90 days from the date of approval. After 90 days, they will expire automatically. You can repost the same ad for free to keep it visible. Promoted (Featured or Sponsored) ads have the same 90-day period but receive significantly more visibility during that time.
Can I post multiple ads?+
Yes! You can have up to 10 active listings per account at any time. If you need more, please contact us at support@pamarket.co.zw to discuss your needs — we're happy to help businesses manage larger catalogues.
🛡️ Account & Safety
How do I get verified?+
Verified status signals to buyers that you're a trusted seller. To get verified, go to your Dashboard → Profile Settings and follow the identity verification steps. You'll need to submit a valid Zimbabwe national ID or passport. Verification is reviewed within 1–2 business days.
How do I report a suspicious listing?+
On any listing page, scroll down to the seller contact section and click "Report this ad." Select the reason for reporting (fraud, prohibited item, misleading information, etc.) and submit. Our moderation team reviews all reports within 24 hours. You can also email support@pamarket.co.zw directly.
I forgot my password — how do I reset it?+
Click "Sign In" in the header, then select "Forgot Password" below the login form. Enter the email address associated with your account and we'll send you a password reset link. Check your spam folder if you don't see it within a few minutes.
How do I block a user?+
Go to your Messages section and open the conversation with the user you'd like to block. Tap the three-dot menu (or the user's name at the top of the chat) and select "Block User." Blocked users cannot contact you or view your listings. You can unblock at any time from your Profile Settings.
💳 Payments & Wallet
How does the PaMarket Wallet work?+
The PaMarket Wallet lets you add funds to your account which can be used to boost and promote your listings. Add money via EcoCash, OneMoney, or bank transfer, then use your balance to purchase Featured or Sponsored placements for your ads from the Promotions section of your Dashboard.
What payment methods are accepted?+
We currently accept: EcoCash, OneMoney, and Zimbabwe bank transfer. We're actively working to add more payment options. All payments are processed securely, and your Wallet balance is available immediately after payment confirmation.
How do I boost my ad?+
Go to your Dashboard → My Ads and click the "Promote" button next to the listing you'd like to boost. Choose between Featured (pinned to top of results) or Sponsored (shown throughout browse results). Select your duration, pay with your Wallet balance, and your promotion goes live instantly.
Are there refunds on promotions?+
If you're unsatisfied with a promotion, please contact our support team at support@pamarket.co.zw within 7 days of purchase. Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We cannot offer refunds for promotions that have already been active and received impressions.
